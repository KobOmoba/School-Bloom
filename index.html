❓ FAQ
How do I add students?

Go to Students tab → tap ➕ Add Student. Or tap CSV Import to upload many at once. CSV format: Name, Phone, Class, Fee.

How do I generate a report card?

Go to Students → tap a student → tap Scores tab → enter CA and Exam scores → tap Report Card tab → tap Print.

Why is my data not syncing?

Check your internet connection. The app works offline and syncs automatically when connected. Look at the dot in the top header — green means online.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Go to Settings tab → tap Upgrade to Premium → contact AariNAT via WhatsApp to arrange payment.

No other party has access to your school's data.
How Long We Keep Your Data
Data is retained for the duration of your active subscription, plus a maximum of 24 months after subscription ends, to allow for account reactivation and any legal or audit requirements.

After this period, all school data is permanently deleted from our systems.

📋 NDPC Registration Process (For AariNAT Admin)
Step 1 — Create Account
Visit ndpc.gov.ng → click "Register" → create a profile for AariNAT Company Limited as a Data Controller.

Step 2 — Determine Registration Category
EduBloom processes children's data (sensitive category) and parent contact data across multiple schools. This likely qualifies AariNAT as a Data Controller of Major Importance (DCMI) once the platform reaches significant scale. At Stage 1–2 (under 50 schools), standard registration applies.

Step 3 — File Data Controller Registration
Submit: company name, RC number, contact details, description of data processing activities, categories of data subjects (students, parents, staff), purpose of processing, and your Data Protection Officer (DPO) details.

Step 4 — Appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO)
Required for organisations processing sensitive data (children's data qualifies). The DPO can be Bayo himself at this stage. Contact: +234 814 507 3941.

Step 5 — Annual Compliance Audit
Each year, file a compliance audit return with NDPC confirming that data protection practices are in place. A licensed Data Protection Compliance Organisation (DPCO) can assist with this for a fee.

Cost: Registration is free on the NDPC portal. Annual audit filing through a licensed DPCO typically costs ₦150,000–₦500,000 depending on the firm.

When to register: Before onboarding paying schools. The NDPC's enforcement sweep of September 2025 targeted organisations already in violation — early registration is the right position.
