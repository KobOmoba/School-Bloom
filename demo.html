⚡ How It Works — 5 Steps

Step 1

📱 Agent signs up a school

The agent opens the Agent App, enters the school's details (name, student count, terms), selects the right tier, and submits. The deal goes to admin instantly.

Step 2

✅ Admin approves & sends credentials

Admin reviews the deal in Bloom Portal, approves it, and the school gets a WhatsApp message with their School ID and password.

Step 3

🏫 Teacher logs in & imports students

Teacher opens the School App, enters their School ID and password. They can add students manually, import a CSV, or take a photo of the class list — the app reads names automatically.

Step 4

📊 Record scores, fees & attendance

Enter CA scores and exams per subject. Track who has paid school fees and who owes. Mark attendance daily. The app does all calculations — averages, rankings, report cards.

Step 5

🖨️ Print report cards in one tap

At end of term, open the Scorecard, tap "Print All". Every student gets a fully formatted Nigerian-standard report card with grades, position, CA breakdown, and comments.
